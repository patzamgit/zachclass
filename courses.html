Our courses

Gain confidence and fluency in conversational English.
There is something for everyone, with courses for all skill levels covering everything from the basics of smalltalk to crafting well-formed opinions about complex, topical issues.

Basic conversation

Gain confidence speaking about familiar topics.

Why take this course?
It can be intimidating to speak with a foreigner, no matter how much grammar and vocabulary you've mastered. If you have basic knowledge of English but have not spent much time speaking, this course will help you ease into your first English conversations.

What will you be able to do?
This course covers vocabulary at the CEFR A2 level. You will build confidence while learning to speak about a variety of common, everyday topics. In addition, you will build implicit grammar knowledge as your tutor models correct answers and corrects your mistakes.

Experience Level
Beginner

Course Length
42 lessons

Pre-requisites
You should be able to introduce yourself and say where you're from. If you are not yet comfortable with basic introductions, start with our course "English Conversation 101".

Intermediate Conversation

Express your ideas and opinions

Why take this course?
Looking for some variety in your lesson topics? Immerse yourself in English discussion with this set of engaging topics.

What will you be able to do?
This course covers vocabulary at the CEFR B1-B2 levels. You will work on improving fluency and comprehension by discussing a variety of topics. In addition, you will receive corrections from a native English speaker to help improve your grammatical accuracy.

Experience Level
Intermediate

Course Length
58 lessons

Pre-requisites
You should be able to hold a conversation in English before beginning this course.

Advanced Conversation

Explore complex topics relevant to modern life.

Why take this course?
Challenge yourself with new topics to expand you vocabulary and increase your comfort with the English language.

What will you be able to do?
This course covers vocabulary at the CEFR C1-C2 levels. You will work on gaining fluency by speaking at length about a wide array of topics. In addition, you will receive corrections from a native English speaker to help improve coherence and grammatical accuracy.

Experience Level
Advanced

Course Length
81 lessons

Pre-requisites
You should be able to speak at length about abstract topics before beginning this course.

English for Travel

Practical English to use while traveling!

Why take this course?
Make your next trip easier and more fun by learning Travel English. English is used in international airports and hotels around the world, even in non-English speaking countries. No matter where you go, this course will prepare you to travel with confidence!

What will you be able to do?
This course will teach you the English words and phrases that you need for common travel situations. You will practice navigating the airport, getting a hotel room, and asking for help, along with the language needed for many other travel-related conversations. Your expert tutor will help you feel confident before you ever leave home!

Experience Level
Beginner

Course Length
38 lessons

Business English

The English you need for your work and career

Why take this course?
Understanding business culture and customs is just as important as learning traditional business-related vocabulary. With Cambly's Business English course, you have the chance to learn from expertly-crafted lessons as well as from your tutor's personal experiences.

What will you be able to do?
This course covers the most common vocabulary and grammatical structures needed to do business in English. The course focuses on simulating real-life business situations to build professional communicative competency. You will also learn idioms being used in English-speaking offices across the globe.

Experience Level
Intermediate

Course Length
10 lessons

Pre-requisites
Intermediate level of English proficiency. No experience with Business English required.

Public Speaking

Learn the basics of public speaking

Why take this course?
Public speaking is an important part of many jobs. This course will help you gain the confidence you need to speak in front of an audience in a variety of business situations such as leading a meeting, giving a status report, and making a sale.

What will you be able to do?
This course covers important presentation skills such as articulating clearly, keeping your audience interested, and describing charts and graphs. You will practice useful grammar for each skill, along with professional vocabulary.

Experience Level
Intermediate

Course Length
21 lessons
