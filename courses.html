Our Courses

We offer a variety of English courses for learners at every level. Discover our range of courses below.

General English - Beginner

Build your conversational skills by discussing everyday topics.

Why choose this course?
No matter how much grammar and vocabulary you have learned, speaking to someone in English can be daunting. You will learn the basics of conversational English in this course, designed for students with a fundamental knowledge of English but not much speaking experience.

What will you be able to do?
You will grow more confident speaking about an array of common, everyday topics, developing crucial grammar knowledge as your teacher corrects your mistakes, and learning vocabulary at a CEFR A2 level.

Experience Level
Beginner

NOTE:
Before starting this course, you should be able to introduce yourself and say where you're from.

General English - Intermediate

Enhance your conversational skills through a variety of engaging topics.

Why choose this course?
Do you know how to discuss everyday topics in English but struggle with unfamiliar ones? You will learn how to talk about a range of subjects with this course, designed for students who want to have more engaging conversations.

What will you be able to do?
You will develop your ability to express thoughts and opinions about various topics, improve your grammar as your teacher corrects your mistakes, and learn vocabulary at CEFR B1-B2 levels.

Experience Level
Intermediate

NOTE:
Before starting this course, you should be capable of having a conversation in English.

General English - Advanced

Fine-tune your ability to discuss complicated topics related to modern society.

Why choose this course?
Do you want to discuss challenging topics to become a more confident English speaker? This course is for people who wish to express themselves in greater detail to be more precise in their speech.

What will you be able to do?
You will increase your fluency by discussing complex modern-day issues, improving your grammar as your teacher corrects your mistakes, and learning vocabulary at CEFR C1-C2 levels.

Experience Level
Advanced

NOTE:
Before starting this course, you should be capable of discussing abstract topics.

Travel English

Acquire the English necessary to travel with confidence.

Why choose this course?
Do you wish to travel with the ability to communicate effectively in English? Even in countries where English is not the primary language, English is still widely used to communicate in hotels, airports, and other tourist destinations. Travel English is an ideal course for people who want to be confident speaking English for their next trip.

What will you be able to do?
You will learn how to communicate in travel-related scenarios such as finding your way through an airport, booking a hotel room, asking for assistance, and other typical travel situations. You will acquire essential vocabulary and phrases to make traveling a breeze.

Experience Level
Beginner

Business English

Develop English language skills required in the office and other business settings.

Why choose this course?
Do you work in an industry where Business English is crucial? How we communicate in our professional lives differs from how we speak to friends and family. This course will help you understand this difference and teach you how to communicate appropriately within a professional context.

What will you be able to do?
You will learn essential vocabulary and phrases to communicate within a business environment. Your teacher will also take you through role-playing scenarios to strengthen the skills necessary to deal with real-world business situations.

Experience Level
Intermediate

NOTE:
Previous experience with Business English is not required.

Public Speaking

Learn the fundamentals of speaking in public.

Why choose this course?
Do you work in a job that requires some level of public speaking? This course will help you improve your communication skills, express your ideas clearly, and keep your audience's attention. You will develop the confidence needed to speak in front of people in several professional situations, including conducting presentations, leading a meeting, and pitching ideas.

What will you be able to do?
You will develop essential presentation skills such as speaking clearly, keeping your audience engaged, and describing visual aids such as charts and graphs. You will also learn the necessary grammar and vocabulary to communicate your ideas successfully.

Experience Level
Intermediate

IELTS Exam Preparation

Achieve your desired IELTS score.

Why choose this course?
This course will help you improve your English and develop your exam techniques, considering all four parts of the IELTS exam: listening, reading, writing, and speaking.

What will you be able to do?
You will learn how to achieve your desired IELTS score by becoming familiar with the exam format, practicing exam questions, and receiving valuable advice from teachers with extensive knowledge of IELTS exam preparation.

Experience Level
Any level

Pre-requisites
If your previous IELTS score is 5 or lower, start with our "General English - Beginner" course.
