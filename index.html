How it works

To find the perfect English course for you at ZachClass, you must first complete a live evaluation to assess your English level.

1

Schedule your live evaluation

Complete the sign-up form and select a time that you are available for your evaluation call.

2

Complete the pre-session quiz

You must complete the pre-session quiz before your scheduled evaluation call. This quiz will assess your grammar, vocabulary, and phrasing. Your results will be discussed during your evaluation call.

3

Review your evaluation report

After your evaluation call, you will receive an evaluation report that will include your English grade based on the CEFR, notes from your evaluator, and an audio recording of the evaluation.

What is your refund policy?

All your unscheduled sessions will be refunded. All future sessions within 48 hours of the refund request are refundable. Past sessions (missed or attended) are non-refundable.

How can I pay my tuition fee?

All major credit or debit cards can be used for all transactions on ZachClass. PayPal and wire transfer are also an accepted form of payment.

How do I reschedule a live session?

From your student account, go to the postpone page, click on the session from the calendar and click on any blue cell at the time/date that is convenient for you.

Can I have more than one teacher?

Yes! When you schedule your session, you can add as many teachers you want. You always can change your teacher. Every teachers can view the lesson(s) content, homework and exercises you had with other teacher(s).
